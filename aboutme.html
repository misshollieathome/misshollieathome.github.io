About Me

I wear many hats: author, webmaster, speaker, supporter and 
many, many more.

My love for pageantry started, like many women, when I was a little girl. I so wanted a sparkly crown on my head and for a large audience to applaud me. I finally got to go behind the scenes in November 2000 and haven't looked back.

My love for communication started when I was a teen. I've had radio shows of my own, and have competed as a public speaker. I've been a member of Toastmasters since 2003 and have supported many people, both in and out of the organization, on their journeys to become better communicators and leaders. 

A few miscellaneous and fun facts: I'm a native born and raised New Orleanian. I hold an amateur radio license. I'm a huge fan of science fiction. Frogs are my lucky charms. And I would love to help you on your pageant journey!
